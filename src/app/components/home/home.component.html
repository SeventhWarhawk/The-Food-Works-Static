DELIVERED TO YOUR DOOR

The Food Works offers you a friendly and reliable delivery to your doorstep service, and also offers in-store order collection when nobody is at home. Customers find delight in using our mobile App to place their orders and arrange delivery all in one place. Discover convenience at the tap of a button. All deliveries hold a R30 service fee.

OUR PROMISE OF QUALITY

Only the highest quality ingredients and standards go behind our meal preparation. The Food Works prides itself in having uncompromised attention to detail and food safety, ensuring that we are not just another food brand. We’re committed to your family’s food journey and are here to help if you have any questions or concerns. Give us a call or send us an email to get in touch. We’re online from 8am to 5pm Mon-Fri and Saturdays from 9am to 1pm. Quality food and quality customer service guaranteed!

EASY. HEALTHY. COST EFFECTIVE

A core value at The Food Works is that we care about YOUR convenience. Our meals are perfectly portion-controlled, well-suited for families which helps to limit waste and means you’ll only pay for what you and your family will eat and enjoy. Our meals come in containers that are suitable for the oven and/or microwave, with simple cooking instructions and great complementary dish suggestions for any occasion.
